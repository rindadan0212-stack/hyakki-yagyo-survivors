# Environment and Spirit-Power Effects

## Stage atmosphere

- Mori: root lines, leaf flecks, deep canopy silhouettes, bamboo landmarks,
  green moon shafts, fireflies, and drifting petals.
- Miyako: cracked stone paving, distant torii gates, violet moon haze, thin
  horizontal mist bands, and pale drifting petals.
- Yomi: ember-lit ground cracks, bone fragments, grave markers, higanbana,
  rising ash and wisps, red underglow, and distant miasma spikes.

All scenery is generated deterministically around the camera. No persistent
decoration entities are allocated.

## Power effects

The shared FX system provides:

- Ground-projected rotating ritual diagrams
- Vertical spirit-light columns
- Expanding impact rings
- Converging sparks and radial particles
- Short screen-edge chromatic pulses

Major ultimates, active skills, bombs, boss appearances and enrages, lantern
ignition, lantern surges, and three-lantern resonance use tailored colors,
scales, glyph counts, and durations.

FX arrays have fixed upper bounds and the engine retains its adaptive
resolution fallback for heavy late-game scenes.
